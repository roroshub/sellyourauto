HERO VIDEO SETUP
================

Place your hero background video file here as:
  /public/hero-video.mp4    (primary — H.264, recommended)
  /public/hero-video.webm   (optional fallback — VP9, smaller file)

RECOMMENDED FREE SOURCES:
  - Pexels.com  → search "car driving" or "luxury car" → Download free
  - Mixkit.co   → search "car" → Free HD stock video
  - Coverr.co   → search "automotive"

RECOMMENDED VIDEO SPECS:
  - Resolution: 1920×1080 (1080p) or higher
  - Duration: 15–30 seconds (seamless loop preferred)
  - File size: Keep under 15MB for fast loading
  - Content: Driving footage, car close-ups, Canadian roads — anything premium

The <video> tag already has:
  autoPlay muted loop playsInline

The site shows a navy blue gradient fallback until the video loads.

OG IMAGE:
Place /public/og-image.jpg (1200×630px) for social sharing previews.

LOGO:
Place /public/logo.png — your SellYourAuto.ca logo file.
Current navbar references this file. Recommended: ~360×96px PNG with transparency.
